Shutterfly Invitations

Shutterfly Invitations is a leading online brand specializing in customizable invitation cards for all occasions, including anniversary celebrations. Known for its high-quality prints and easy-to-use design tools, Shutterfly allows users to personalize invitations with photos, themes, fonts, and colors, making every invite unique and memorable. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone anniversary or an intimate gathering, Shutterfly combines elegance and convenience, offering both digital and printed options that cater to modern and traditional styles alike.

Greetings Island

Greetings Island is a popular online platform that lets you create beautiful, customizable anniversary invitations with ease. It offers a wide variety of professionally designed templates that can be personalized with names, photos, dates, colors, and fonts to perfectly match your celebration’s theme. You can choose to download and print your invitations, send them digitally via email or social media, or even create an online event page with RSVP tracking to manage your guests. With both free and premium options, Greetings Island makes designing elegant and memorable anniversary invites simple and convenient for every type of celebration.

Click Invitation

Click Invitation is a modern digital invitation and event management platform designed to make creating and sharing anniversary invitations (as well as invites for weddings, birthdays, and other celebrations) simple, stylish, and stress‑free. With Click Invitation, you can choose from a wide range of professionally designed anniversary templates and customize every detail — from colors, fonts, and photos to event wording — so your invitation perfectly reflects your celebration’s theme and tone.

Wizzart Prints

Wizzart Prints is an online brand that offers elegantly crafted invitation cards, including anniversary invitations, designed to make your special celebration truly memorable. Their collection features a range of premium options — from classic printed cards to more eye‑catching acrylic invitations with sophisticated touches like gold foil and custom detailing. Each invitation can be personalized with your event information and styled to match your theme, helping you set the perfect tone for your anniversary event. With a focus on quality materials, creative designs, and customization, Wizzart Prints aims to deliver invitations that reflect the spirit of your celebration and leave a lasting impression on your guests.
